Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our resume builder service:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and location
  • Professional Information: Work experience, education, skills, and certifications
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely through Razorpay

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our resume building service

  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted partners like Razorpay for payment processing

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure payment processing through certified providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of communications

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
